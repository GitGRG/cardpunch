CONTROLS [MOUSE/TOUCH]

  • - Click/Tap decks to draw cards to hand
  • - Drag cards into the play area
  • - Click/Tap Corner of played cards for larger view
  • - Right Click or Long Press cards to discard back to decks
  • - Drag markers and dice counters
  • - Click dice or tap edge to change their values
  • - Right Click or tap dice to roll random
